About RAV Group

RAV deals with the distribution of a wide range of high-quality Real Estate Projects and lifestyle products for day-to-day life. We aim to deliver the best products directly to our customers, who form the core of the company. Our sales team of registered Associates are trained leaders and representatives who ensure that the consumers get the best of every product they desire.

RAV, which started its operations in the year 2012, is now becoming a leading Real Estate company dealing in world-class Real Estate and lifestyle products. The Company was formed to develop well designed, competitively priced as well as luxury real estate Projects in pan-India.

We are pioneering in a few segments that any real estate company has never touched before. Our "Country side Real Estate" segment in which we focus on developing farm houses, villas and other residential/commercial projects is for the people who love to live close to the nature. RAV is also focusing on Greenfield Smart Cities to enhance industrialization and manufacturing and creating jobs. Our initiative in this segment is "RAV Green City" in Dholera, Gujarat.

Third segment where we aim to cater Theme based commercial and residential luxury projects is enthusiastically being taken by our investors worldwide. Here we merge trending and growing industries with real estate. Our project "The River Castle” is a combination of top three industries, Wellness/Health, Hospitality & Real Estate. This real estate model gives an opportunity to the investors to invest and earn life time revenue.

RAV is constantly expanding its product range and believes in providing world-class services to all its customers. It has been emerging in the sectors like Hospitality, Advertisement & Media, IT & Conceptual daily need products.

As Virginia Woolf said "One cannot think well, love well, sleep well, if one has not dined well". Keeping that in mind RAV has started an organic revolution with a 200+ acre Organic farm at Naimisharanya and more than five others are in line. Chain of an Organic Food Court(Bhoj Organica) is also on the way to meet our motto, and that is "Let food be thy medicine, thy medicine shall be thy food"

RAV is constantly growing at a phenomenal rate every year. The growth rate in itself speaks volumes about the quality of the products, the marketing plan and the management that have been able to deliver such a rewarding and sustainable system.
