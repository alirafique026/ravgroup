About Us

Marching in the direction of being a Global Leader, RAV Group is a well-mounted impartial organization, founded in 2012, encompassing a huge area within side the region of real-estate and E-commerce companies. RAV Group has had an incredible journey throughout its years of operation. Grouping collectively Real-Estate, E-commerce, Foundation, Organics, Skyscrapers, Resorts, etc., RAV stands atop in its league. Bringing forth the wishes of its personnel and making the surroundings appropriate for them, is one of the high concerns of the RAV Group.

RAV GLOBAL Solutions Pvt Ltd. deals with the distribution of a wide range of high-quality Real Estate Projects and lifestyle products for the day to day life. We aim to deliver the best products directly to our customers, who form the core of the company. Our sales team of registered Associates are trained leaders and representatives who ensure that the consumers get the best lifestyle products and the services, with additional Free Business Opportunity benefits. The profitable opportunities offered have influenced many customers to purchase products from non-retail environments, owing to the expansion of business across the country.
