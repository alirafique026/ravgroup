Who We Are

RAV Global Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a Real-Estate based company which started off as a broker firm. Since the commencement of the company, its soul purpose was clear to deliver what we promise with utmost perfection and within the deadline. After establishing our presence in Real-Estate, we set our foot in Fashion and Beauty industry through Sundara. Comprising of several other ventures, RAV as a whole is a package which has left no stone unturned, be it Beauty, E-commerce, Organic Farms, Hospitality & Wellness, etc. RAV believes in empowering its members with the opportunity to lead their lives on their terms.
