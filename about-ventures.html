Our Ventures

Grouping collectively Real-Estate, E-commerce, Foundation, Organics, Skyscrapers, Resorts, etc., RAV stands atop in its league. RAV GLOBAL Solutions Pvt. Ltd is constantly expanding its product range and believes in providing world-class services to all its customers. It has been building a wide network of distributors, which is constantly growing every year.

 

 

RAV Organics

RAV Organics is a living embodiment of purity and trust in action. It's a RAV Group project with the goal of providing you with the greatest organic products to suit and calm your body.

 

 

RAV Global Resorts & Holidays Ltd.

RAV Global Resorts & Holidays is a subsidiary of RAV Global that focuses on the company's hospitality sector. RAV Global has adopted to market trends and has established a presence in the business of exotic resorts and organic food courts.

 

 

RAV Skyscraper Pvt. Ltd.

RAV Skyscraper is solely dedicated to offer you unadulterated concrete excellence through proper R&D for devising and designing exclusive projects of varied nature servicing all school of thoughts.

 

 

Club Freedom

RAV Global's Club Freedom is a venture formed exclusively for 'RAViens.' It is a wing that is focused towards providing entertainment and leisure time with family / friends, in India and abroad. This club is solely created to give you freedom to live your life lavishly with hapiness.

 

 

RAV Foundation

RAV Foundation is a non-governmental and non-profitable organization, initiated by Mrs. Rachna Sisodia. Her vision is very distinct to imbibe the youth with clarity to carry out a change in society. The Foundation is led by RAV Global and is considered as an integral part of the company that works for the society.

 

 

RAV Sundara

RAV Group, which is already running its ventures in Real Estate , Natural Products & Hospitality for several years, decided to capture the unorganised fashion Jewelry market with its unique marketing plan. RAV Group launched its beauty venture with it’s brand “SUNDARA” that deals in Designer Fashion Jewelry.

 

 

Viraali Global Media Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Viraali Global Media Solutions is a company of RAV Group, founded in the financial year 2021 - 2022. Under this banner, we offer media solutions concerning the advertising industry. Viraali provides a single window platform where anyone can book his advertisement in any vertical.

 

 

RAV Download

RAV Download is another E-commerce venture of RAV Group. Enjoy shopping from anywhere - anytime with RAV Download. We offer a huge selection of original products from fashion jewellery to personal care, and you can get timely updates on what's new and what's hot.
