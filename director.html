Mrs. Rachna Sisodia

RAV Global Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was founded on the basis of hard work, devotion, and perseverance. It gives me great joy as a director to be a part of a group that is not exclusively male. We provide equal opportunities for everyone to advance and establish themselves in the industry. As a woman, my credo is to "empower other women" and walk the path to success together. Empowering women to make their own decisions, run their own business, and live life on their own term is our mission.

"Feminism isn't about making women stronger. Women are already strong, it's about changing the way the world perceives that strength."
